Capstone II: Accidents Analysis


In this blog post, I cover my second capstone project for the Data Science Career Track, which I graduated from in August 2015. The initial goal of the project was to predict accidents along interstates using characteristics of the road derived from processing files in QGIS, but unfortunately those characteristics were not strongly predictive. I pivoted my project to use data from the Fatal Analysis Reporting System to predict involvement of drunk drivers in fatal accidents. I trained LogisticRegressors and ensemble tree models as well as experimented with SMOTE methods to better train those estimators on imbalanced data.
