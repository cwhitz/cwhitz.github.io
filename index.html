Capstone II: Accidents Analysis - Chester Hitz - Medium

Springboard Data Science Career Track Capstone II Submitted: July 30, 2018 The initial question of this was: Is it possible to predict fatal road accidents in trucking? In this project, I wanted to use two publicly available datasets, the Fatality Analysis Reporting System (FARS) and the Freight Analysis Framework (FAF4) to predict fatal trucking accidents on selected roads.
